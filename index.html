Odikpo Family History

Odikpo Kindred Begins

Ancient Times (1st Generation)

The patriarch of the Odikpo kindred, Onaga, established the family lineage in Eastern Nigeria, founding what would become a large family with many generations.

Founding

Second Generation

Historical Era (2nd Generation)

The second generation of the Odikpo kindred included Oluorhu and Okoji, sons of Onaga, who continued the family legacy in Eastern Nigeria.

Growth of the Kindred

Historical Era (3rd-5th Generations)

Generations 3-5 saw the expansion of the family under Odobianu, Okpalo, and Odikpo himself, for whom the kindred is named.

Family Name

Family Branches Form

Recent Centuries (6th and 7th Generations)

The sixth and seventh generations saw the formation of key family branches under Nneuchendu, Oosikwe, and their children.

Family Branches

Expansion of the Kindred

Modern Era (8th and 9th Generations)

The eighth and ninth generations saw significant growth with numerous descendants spreading across Nigeria.
